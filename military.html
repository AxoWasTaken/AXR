We assume you are here because you are interested in joining (Or just having a look). The highest rank in the military is the Militant Director, to get this rank you must have led a legion during a war or threat of war. Militant Directors are not associated with the main military and they just develop weapons for it, they also advise the Sentry. Then there are Generals, there are 3 generals, each one leading a Legion. The Generals are responsible for planning battle, keeping their legion in-line, and representing their legion to the Sentry. They take their orders directly from the Sentry. Next are commanders, inside of each legion are 3 divisions, so each legion has its own Ground, Sea, and Air force. The commanders lead these divisions, and report the needs of their men to their General. Then below that, are most importantly soldiers, they follow the orders their commanders give them, and protect the AXR.
